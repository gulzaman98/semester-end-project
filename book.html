How to Fold a Traditional Origami Heart Bookmark

The traditional paper origami heart bookmark is a simple and creative origami design, specially made to mark pages in books. It is shaped like a heart, symbolizing love and care for reading. This easy origami model is perfect for kids and beginners, helping them learn basic folding techniques while creating something useful and decorative.

Traditional Paper Heart Bookmark Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Cut a square sheet of paper in half.
  2. Start with the white side facing up. Fold the paper in half horizontally and make a pinch mark at the right edge. Then unfold.
  3. Fold the top and bottom edges to meet the pinch mark. Make two new pinch marks at the left and right edges, then unfold.
  4. Fold the top edge to the nearest pinch mark. Then unfold.
  5. Fold the top edge to the crease you just made. Add two pinch marks at the left and right edges, then unfold.
  6. Fold the paper in half vertically. Make a pinch mark at both the top and bottom edges.
    Note: The top edge pinch mark should extend slightly past the crease from Step 4.
  7. Fold the top edge down to the second pinch mark (counting from the top).
  8. Turn the paper over.
  9. Fold the top left and right edges in to meet the center pinch mark from Step 6.
  10. Focusing on the top left flap, fold its bottom left edge to the center pinch mark. Repeat on the right flap.
  11. Mountain fold the top section along the top edges of the flaps you just made in Step 10.
  12. Open the top layer and squash fold it upward as shown.
  13. Move the flap from back to front, reversing the fold you made in Step 10.
  14. Fold the tip of the triangle downward.
  15. Repeat Steps 12 to 14 on the right side.
  16. Fold the left and right edges in to meet the center pinch mark at the bottom edge.
  17. Turn the model over.
  18. Fold the bottom left edge to the center pinch mark. Crease well, then unfold.
  19. Inside reverse fold along the crease you just made in Step 18.
  20. Repeat Steps 18 and 19 on the right side.
  21. Your origami heart bookmark is ready!
