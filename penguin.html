How to Fold a Traditional Origami Crane

The traditional paper origami penguin is a fun and popular origami model, especially loved by kids. It is inspired by the cute penguin bird found in cold regions. This easy origami design helps children learn basic folding skills while improving focus and creativity. The origami penguin is a great way to enjoy paper art and develop patience through simple step-by-step folds.
