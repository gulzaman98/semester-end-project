Traditional Paper Celebration Crane Step-by-Step Instructions

    ```html
  1. Start with a square sheet of paper, colored side up, and place it flat on the table.
  2. Fold the paper diagonally in half to form a triangle, then unfold to create a crease.
  3. Fold diagonally in the opposite direction and unfold to form a second crease.
  4. Fold the paper in half horizontally and vertically, then unfold to create a grid of creases.
  5. Collapse the paper into a preliminary base (square base) using the creases.
  6. Fold the right and left edges toward the center to form a kite shape.
  7. Turn the model over and repeat the folds on the other side.
  8. Fold the top point downward slightly to start shaping the crane’s body.
  9. Lift the top layer and squash fold to form the head and neck of the crane.
  10. Make a small inside reverse fold at the tip of the neck to shape the crane’s beak.
  11. Fold the sides outward slightly to form the wings, giving them a graceful curve.
  12. Extend the back flap upward to create the tail of the crane.
  13. Adjust the neck, head, and wings so the crane looks elegant and balanced.
  14. Refine all creases carefully for a clean and precise finish.
  15. Gently spread the wings to display the crane in a flying position.
  16. Your Celebration Crane origami is now complete and ready for display.
