How to Fold a Traditional Origami Herat

The traditional paper origami heart is a simple yet charming design that represents love, care, and affection. This model is perfect for beginners and advanced folders alike, offering a fun and creative way to make personalized gifts, cards, or decorations. Folding the origami heart helps improve precision, patience, and hand-eye coordination, while resulting in a delightful and meaningful paper creation.

Traditional Paper Heart Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Start with a square piece of paper, colored side facing up.
  2. Fold the paper in half diagonally to form a triangle.
  3. Unfold and fold diagonally the opposite direction.
  4. Turn the paper over to the other side.
  5. Fold the paper in half horizontally.
  6. Unfold and fold vertically.
  7. Collapse the paper into a preliminary triangle base.
  8. Position the triangle with the open end at the bottom.
  9. Fold the right corner up to meet the top point.
  10. Fold the left corner up to meet the top point.
  11. Turn the model over.
  12. Fold the top layer down slightly past the bottom edge.
  13. Crease firmly and hold the fold.
  14. Fold the left and right flaps inward to meet at the center line.
  15. Turn the model over again.
  16. Fold the top points down to round the top of the heart.
  17. Fold the side edges slightly inward to shape the heart curves.
  18. Flip the model back to the front side.
  19. Adjust the top flaps for a smooth curved edge.
  20. Fold the bottom tip upward slightly to round the point.
  21. Flatten all creases carefully.
  22. Check symmetry of both sides of the heart.
  23. Refine the edges for a neat appearance.
  24. Press the center fold firmly.
  25. Adjust the curves for a balanced shape.
  26. Flatten any uneven areas along the edges.
  27. Shape the heart gently with your fingers for 3D effect.
  28. Make small adjustments to the top lobes for evenness.
  29. Ensure all folds are crisp and sharp.
  30. Your origami heart is now complete and ready to display!
